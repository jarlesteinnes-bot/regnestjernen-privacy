Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-04-21

1. Summary

Stella Math / Regnestjernen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data stays on the device. This app is made for kids and complies with COPPA, GDPR, and the UK Age Appropriate Design Code.

2. What data we collect

None. The app never sends data to us or anyone else.

3. What is stored on the device

The following information is stored locally on the device only (using iOS UserDefaults and local files):

  • Selected profile name (your choice — it can be anything).
  • Avatar choice.
  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned).
  • Subscription state.
  • App settings.

None of this leaves the device.

4. Subscription

The subscription is handled by StoreKit and Apple. We do not see your name, email, or payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs the transaction.

5. Third parties

None. No SDKs, no analytics, no ads, no social integrations, no network calls at all.

6. Children's privacy

The app is listed in the Kids category and complies with:

  • COPPA (US) — no data collection from children under 13.
  • GDPR and GDPR-K (EU/EEA) — no personal data processed.
  • UK Age Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code) — highest privacy defaults.
  • Australian Privacy Principles.
  • PIPEDA (Canada).
  • LGPD (Brazil).
  • APPI (Japan).
  • PIPA (Korea).
  • Norwegian Personopplysningsloven.

7. Parental controls

The parent panel is protected by a 4-digit PIN. Parents control profiles, subscription, and progress resets.

8. Your rights

Since no data ever leaves the device, there is nothing to request or delete from us. Uninstalling the app removes all data.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@regnestjernen.no.

10. Changes to this policy

We will update the date at the top of this page when the policy changes. Material changes required by compliance obligations will also be reflected in the app.
